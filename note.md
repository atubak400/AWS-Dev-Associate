Private clouds

Private clouds provide greater control for the company and its IT department compared to public clouds. This increased control allows for more customized security, compliance, and performance configurations, catering to the specific needs of the organization. However, this often comes at the expense of higher costs and less general public availability, as the infrastructure is dedicated to a single organization and managed internally or by a dedicated third-party provider.
